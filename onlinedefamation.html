Online Defamation
Internet defamation generally involves the online publication of a false statement of fact that is harmful to one's reputation. There may be some exceptions or additional elements of showing malice where the plaintiff is a public figure.

What are the types of online defamation? Are you a victim of it?

    There are two types of online defamation: Libel and Slander.

  • Slander: Slander is the verbal dissemination of defamatory statements. For instance, in the event that an individual offers misleading expressions about you in a discourse, it will be de-fame. A podcast or video is another option for this.
  • Libel: Libel is the spreading of defamation through written statements. Libel, for instance, refers to the publication of a defamatory article about you.
